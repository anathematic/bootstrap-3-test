TASTE:
A classic but not-too-thick taste with a great spicy kick, tasted like bottle tomato juice but not the cheap stuff. Could probably drink like 3 in a row, and at the time I won't lie, may have contemplate it...
VENUE:
We came on a Monday mid afternoon and the place was surprisingly busy. Menu was awesome, as was the fit out and decor. Watch those pesky wasps outside (the only thing that put a downer to the experience) will definitely come back here to try BMS with breakfast - research...
FUNSTUFF:
Beef Jerky, cucumber and lemon wedge.
RATING:
4/5
