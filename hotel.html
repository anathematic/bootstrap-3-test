Hawaii Prince Hotel Waikiki

The Hawaii Prince Hotel Waikiki is a luxury hotel, housed in a fantastic architectural design building. It enjoys a great location at the centre of the Ala Wai yacht harbor and is very close to Ala Moana shopping center, which is a great shopping, eating and leisure area. The hotel is only 15 minutes' walk from the bustle of Waikiki district. The resort provides seafront guest rooms and a sublime setting along with the stylish comfort of a luxury resort. The distance to Honolulu airport is approximately 15 km.
